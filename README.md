# 🚀 Visionix AI

### AI-Powered Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Platform

Visionix AI is an intelligent data analytics platform designed to automate the complete data analysis workflow, from data cleaning and preprocessing to visualization, machine learning, and business insight generation.

The platform enables users to upload datasets and instantly receive automated analytics, interactive dashboards, predictive models, and AI-generated insights without requiring extensive technical expertise.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Organizations often spend significant time cleaning data, performing exploratory analysis, building dashboards, and extracting business insights.

Visionix AI simplifies this process by providing an intelligent platform capable of transforming raw datasets into actionable intelligence within minutes.
